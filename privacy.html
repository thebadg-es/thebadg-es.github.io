Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 9, 2026

This privacy policy explains how thebadg.es handles information on this website and in the InfoBadge2040 configurator.

Summary

  • We do not collect or store personal data.
  • We do not use cookies.
  • We do not sell personal data.
  • We do not run third-party advertising trackers.
  • InfoBadge2040 data stays in your browser unless you share it.

Information We Collect

This website does not collect or store any personal information. We do not use analytics or tracking services.

If you contact us by email, we receive your email address and the information you include in the message, but this is not stored on our website.

InfoBadge2040 Configurator

The InfoBadge2040 configurator runs entirely in your browser. The name, role, company, social handle, and QR URL are used to generate a UART payload. We do not transmit this data to our servers.

When you connect a badge over UART, the data is sent directly from your browser to the device using Web Serial. thebadg.es does not receive or store this data.

Cookies

This website does not use cookies of any kind. No cookies are stored on your device when you visit this site.

Data Retention

We do not store any data on our servers. Email communications are handled through standard email services and are not retained by thebadg.es.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store personal data on this website, there is no data to access, correct, or delete. If you have questions, contact us at hello@thebadg.es.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a new "Last updated" date.

Contact

Questions about privacy can be sent to hello@thebadg.es.
